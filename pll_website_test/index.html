Welcome!
I am a mathematician, currently assistant professor in the logic group at Stockholm University.
My research lies broadly in category theory, logic, & constructive mathematics. Most of my recent work is within or related to the Homotopy Type Theory/Univalent Foundations programme: developing dependent type theory as a foundation for mathematics, drawing on ideas from homotopy theory & higher category theory. I am also interested in quantum programming languages (I worked on the early stages of Quipper) & mathematical computing. For more information, see my publications and selected talks.
